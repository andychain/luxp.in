Luxpin is a curated directory of trusted crypto platforms — built to help players find safe, legitimate places to play.

## We cut through the noise.

The online crypto platform space moves fast — and not always in players' favour. New sites launch overnight, promise outsized returns, flashy bonuses, and mystery box jackpots that seem too good to be true. Many of them are. They appear, take deposits, and disappear just as quickly, leaving players with nowhere to turn.

Luxpin exists because that shouldn't be the norm.

## What we do

We research, vet, and list crypto platforms that are worth your time and your trust. Every platform in our directory is evaluated before it earns a spot — we look at licensing, payout history, ownership transparency, and community reputation. If a platform doesn't hold up, it doesn't make the list.

We don't chase volume. We don't list everything. We list what we'd actually recommend.

## Why it matters

Fake casinos and short-lived mystery box platforms have become a real problem. They're built to look credible, engineered to lure with unrealistic rewards, and designed to disappear before accountability catches up. Players lose funds, lose trust, and often have no recourse.

Our directory is a filter. A place where the work of vetting has already been done, so you can focus on finding a platform that suits you — not figuring out whether it's legitimate.

## How we stay honest

We're supported by affiliate partnerships with the platforms we list. When you visit a platform through Luxpin and sign up, we may earn a commission. This is how the directory stays free and independent.

Our listings are editorially independent. Platforms cannot pay to be listed, cannot pay to improve their placement, and are removed if standards slip. Affiliate income follows quality — not the other way around.

## Built for Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and India

Luxpin is localised for each market we cover. Platforms are matched to regions based on licensing, currency support, and legal availability. What you see is relevant to where you are.
