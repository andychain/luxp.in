## Advertising placements.

We offer banner ad placements across the following pages:

- **Homepage** (Canada, Australia, New Zealand, The United Kingdom, The Unite States, and India) — the primary entry point for visitors in each market.
- **Top Picks** — our highest-traffic curated page, visited by users looking for the best-rated platforms.
- **Hot** — platforms currently trending with strong community interest.
- **New** — freshly added platforms, visited by users looking for what's just launched.

All placements are clearly marked as advertising in accordance with our disclosure policy.

## What we don't do.

We do not sell editorial listings. Platforms cannot pay to be added to the directory or to improve their ranking. Advertising and editorial are kept separate. If you're interested in a listing rather than a banner placement, see [How We Rate](/how-we-rate).
