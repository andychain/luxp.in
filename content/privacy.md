## 1. Introduction

Luxpin ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Automatically collected data:** When you visit the Site, we may collect standard log data including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs. This is collected via Google Analytics and is used solely for aggregate traffic analysis.

**No account registration:** Luxpin does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information directly.

## 3. Cookies

We use cookies to support site functionality and analytics. See our [Cookie Policy](/cookies) for a full breakdown of the cookies we use and how to manage them.

## 4. Affiliate Tracking

When you click a referral link to a third-party platform, that platform may place its own cookies on your device to track signups and attribute commissions. These cookies are governed by the respective platform's own privacy policy.

## 5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Site. Google Analytics collects anonymised usage data. You can opt out via the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data for analytical or business purposes.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain server log data for a maximum of 90 days. Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention settings.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of data held about you. To exercise these rights, contact us via [Telegram](https://t.me/LuxpinChat).

## 9. Third-Party Sites

This policy applies only to Luxpin. Platforms linked from this Site have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review before signing up.

## 10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
