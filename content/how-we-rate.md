Every platform on Luxpin earns its place. Nothing is listed by default, and nothing stays listed without meeting our standards. Here's exactly how we evaluate each one.

## Safety comes first — always.

Before anything else, a platform must prove it's safe to use. Players should be able to deposit, play, and withdraw without risk, trouble, or uncertainty.

We look at whether the platform holds a recognised licence, how long it has been operating, who owns and operates it, and whether there is a credible history of paying players out. We check community reputation across multiple sources, looking for patterns of delayed withdrawals, closed accounts without cause, or unresolved disputes.

If a platform raises doubts on safety — no matter how polished it looks — it does not make the list.

## Then user experience.

Once safety is satisfied, we assess the experience of actually using the platform. We expect it to be top notch.

This means a clean, responsive interface that works across devices. Fast and reliable performance. A straightforward registration and verification process. Deposit and withdrawal flows that are smooth, not buried in friction. Responsive customer support that actually resolves issues. And platform features — games, trading tools, mystery boxes — that work as advertised.

A platform that is safe but frustrating to use will rank lower than one that is both safe and excellent to use.

## Bonuses and rewards.

We assess bonus offers critically. We look at wagering requirements, withdrawal limits tied to bonuses, and whether the terms are transparent and reasonable. Platforms that dangle unrealistic offers with impossible conditions are scored down.

## Our badge system.

Platforms that pass our evaluation may receive one of three badges:

- **★ Top Pick** — meets the highest bar across safety, experience, and overall value.
- **🔥 Hot** — currently seeing strong activity, new promotions, or increased community interest.
- **✦ New** — recently added to the directory and meeting our entry standards.

Badges are reviewed regularly and can be removed or changed as platforms evolve.
