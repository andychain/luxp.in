## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make sites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the Site to function. They include cookies that remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). These cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `data-theme` | Stores your light/dark mode preference | Session |

### Analytics Cookies
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. These cookies collect anonymised information such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `_ga` | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Stores session state | 1 year |

### Affiliate Tracking Cookies
When you click a referral link on Luxpin, the destination platform may set its own cookies to track your signup and attribute a commission to us. These cookies are set by third-party platforms, not by Luxpin, and are governed by those platforms' own cookie and privacy policies.

## Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this Site or third-party platforms.

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
